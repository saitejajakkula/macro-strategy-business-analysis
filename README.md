# Macro Strategy Business Analysis

Strategic analysis of how macroeconomic forces influence business models, industry profitability, and competitive strategy.

## Project Overview
This project explores how macroeconomic variables such as inflation, interest rates, and GDP growth shape business outcomes across industries. The analysis connects macro signals to strategic decisions using frameworks, real-world case studies, and data-driven insights.

## Key Topics Covered
- Macro → Strategy → Business Outcomes framework
- Business model sensitivity to macro cycles
- Pricing power and inflation resilience
- Industry structure using Porter’s Five Forces
- Case studies across Technology, Airlines, and FMCG sectors
- Second-order thinking in macro analysis
- Strategic risk management and macro dashboards

## Key Insights
- Inflation, interest rates, and GDP growth explain a majority of business performance across sectors.
- Pricing power is the strongest defense against inflation shocks.
- Asset-light business models perform better during high interest rate environments.
- Firms that adapt strategy early to macro signals outperform competitors over the long run.
